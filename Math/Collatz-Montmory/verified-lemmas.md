## Baseline Arithmetic Facts

The following baseline facts may be used once definitions are fixed:

1. If `n` is a positive even integer, then `n / 2` is a positive integer.
2. If `n` is a positive odd integer, then `3n + 1` is a positive even integer.
3. Therefore the classical Collatz map sends positive integers to positive integers.

These are elementary properties of the classical map, not evidence for the full Collatz conjecture or for any Montmory variant.
