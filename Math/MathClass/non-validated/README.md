# MathClass Non-Validated Area

This directory contains the MathClass research programme, reports, PDF, scans,
and interpretation notes.

## Status

The finite identities tested by the scripts are exact at finite `N`, but the
larger claim that terminal signatures give a validated asymptotic mathematics
of arithmetic classes is **not validated**.

In particular, this area does not prove:

- Collatz;
- Hardy-Littlewood;
- infinitely many twin primes;
- existence of terminal limits for primes, twins, cousins, or sexy primes;
- a special role for `B=89`;
- a twin-specific Montmory filter.

## Current Verdict

The large scan up to `N=3,000,000` indicates:

- the finite MathClass identities work;
- prime-pattern distances to controls decrease with `N`;
- `A={14,23,61,89}` does not isolate twin primes;
- no twin-specific terminal resonance is validated.
